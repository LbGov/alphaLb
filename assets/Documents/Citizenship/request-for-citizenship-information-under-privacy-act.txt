You will need to print and sign this form.

Application for information under the Privacy Act 1993

This application form is for use by people wanting to find out about their own citizenship status, or the citizenship status of their child. If the child is 16 years of age or older, they must request the information themselves.

Applications take up to 20 working days to process.

You can send this form to us by scanning it and emailing it to Citizenship@dia.govt.nz, or posting to Citizenship Privacy Requests, PO Box 10680, Wellington 6143, New Zealand.
