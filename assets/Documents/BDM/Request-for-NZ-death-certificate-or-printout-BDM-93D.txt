You will need to print and sign this form. Digital signatures are not allowed.

Freephone 0800 22 52 52 (+64 4 463 9362 if outside New Zealand).

Definitions:
A certificate is an official document containing registered information.
A printout is a copy of the information from the registration and is not a legal document. A printout is either typed or a copy of
the handwritten entry. Whether a typed or copy of the handwritten entry is issued depends on the record - this is not an option.
