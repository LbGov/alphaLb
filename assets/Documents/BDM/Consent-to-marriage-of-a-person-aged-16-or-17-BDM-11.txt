Internal Affairs Te Tari Taiwhenua

BDM11

Consent to Marriage of a Person aged 16 or 17.
Whakaaetanga Mārena i tētahi Tangata kei ngā tau 16, 17 rānei

Important

1. Normally the consent of both parents is required.
2. Where one parent is deceased, show “Surviving Parent”.
3. Where the parents are living apart and the minor is living with one of them, the consent of that parent only is required. In this case show “Parent with whom minor is living”.
4. Where the minor is in the care of a legal guardian, show “Legal guardian”. In other cases, or if in doubt, consult a Registrar of Marriages.
5. Separate forms should be used when the consent of more than one person is required.
