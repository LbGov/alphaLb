Apply for a new passport

You need a passport to travel overseas. A passport costs 60.000 LBP if for one year and 300.000 LBP if for five years. It usually takes up to three working days

Apply for a new passport online

You can apply online if you're 18 or over and an LB citizen. You need to have:

  • You must be a Lebanese Citizen to apply for a passport.
  • Passport is either valid for one year or for five years.
  • A credit card to pay online

Apply for a child passport

Minors aged 3years and less are granted a passport based on the extract of civil status issued 3 months ago at the most, a copy of the latter, and the above-mentioned documents. The authorization of the parents or the legal guardian is requested.

If you need to travel urgently

To receive the passport immediately, the interested party can go to the bureau of public relations - general security. An additional fee of LBP 105 000 will be however requested.

Modification of your name on the passport

If there is any problem with the associated name on the passport, documents supporting the case must be submitted in order to rectify the issue.

If your passport is lost or stolen

You must report the loss or theft of your passport to the Regional Passport Office so that your passport can be cancelled.

Request to change a passport

A request to change a passport can be granted for any of the following reasons: Missing passport pages , Israeli stamps, Damaged information/photo pages

Damaged passport

When you can't apply online

You can use a paper application form if you can't apply for a passport online. You can't apply online if:

  • You do not possess an online account on the Lebanese Government Portal.
  • You do not possess an e-payment method to pay for the online fees.
  • You are an Expat applying for the passport for the first time.
