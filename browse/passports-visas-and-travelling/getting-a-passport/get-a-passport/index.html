Apply for a new adult passport

You need a passport to travel overseas. Most people can apply for a new passport online. It costs $180 and takes up to 10 working days.

Apply for a new adult passport online

You can apply online if you're 16 or over and an NZ citizen. You need to have:

  • a verified RealMe account — this is a RealMe account where you've been to a NZ Post Shop and had your identify checked and a photo taken
  • a cellphone that is linked to your RealMe account — you will need this to receive a security code to log in
  • a digital passport photograph
  • a credit card or a NZ Post Prezzy Card.

If you need to travel urgently

If you need to travel in a hurry you can apply online for an urgent passport. It costs $360 and is processed within 3 working days.

Urgent travel

If you've changed your name on your passport

If your name has changed since your last passport was issued, you'll have to apply for a new passport. You can apply for a passport online if your name was changed within New Zealand.

Name change — Passports

If your passport is lost or stolen

You must report the loss or theft of your passport to the Passport Office so that your passport can be cancelled.

Lost, stolen or damaged passport

When you can't apply online

You can use a paper application form if you can't apply for a passport online. You can't apply online if:

  • you've ever had a refugee travel document or certificate of identity travel document issued by the Department of Internal Affairs (DIA)
  • the name on your RealMe account doesn't match the name you want on your passport
  • you need an endorsement printed on the observation page of your passport because, for example, your full name has more than 48 characters or you're unable to sign your passport.
