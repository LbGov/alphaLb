What you need to know

You can get a congratulatory message for significant birthdays and wedding anniversaries.

You can get a message for:

  • birthdays of 100 years and over
  • wedding anniversaries — 50th, 55th, 60th, 65th, and 70th and over.

Messages will be sent from Her Majesty the Queen, Her Excellency the Governor-General, the Prime Minister, the Minister of Internal Affairs, the Minister for Seniors and your local MP, depending on what you're celebrating.

You need to apply to get a message. The person celebrating the event can apply, or someone can apply for them.

Messages are posted 1 week before the birthday or wedding anniversary.

This service is free.

Who can get it

All New Zealand citizens and permanent residents who live in New Zealand can get a message.

Before you apply

You need the following information to apply for a congratulatory message:

  • date of birth or marriage
  • if the birth or marriage happened in New Zealand or outside New Zealand — if it was outside New Zealand, please provide a copy of the birth or marriage certificate if possible
  • full name or names written on the certificate
  • preferred first name of the person or people celebrating the event
  • current address for the person or people celebrating the event, including their electorate.

How you apply

You should apply 4 to 8 weeks before the birthday or wedding anniversary. If the event is in January, you should apply no later than the first week of December.

If the Congratulatory Message Service gets your application less than 2 weeks before the anniversary, they will try to send the message before the event but can not guarantee it.

You can not apply if the birthday or anniversary was more than 6 months ago.

Complete the application form

Fill in the form and either email or post it to the Congratulatory Message Service.

Who sees the information

The information you enter in the form will only be shared with the people who are part of this service. This includes DIA staff administering the message service, Her Excellency the Governor-General, the Prime Minister, the Minister of Internal Affairs, the Minister for Seniors and Members of Parliament.

After you apply

The Congratulatory Message Service will email you 2 to 5 working days after they receive your application. They will confirm the details to appear on the message, the delivery address and the date they will send the message.
