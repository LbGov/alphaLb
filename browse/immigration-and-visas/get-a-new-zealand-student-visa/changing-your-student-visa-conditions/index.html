Changing your student visa conditions

If you want to change what or where you're studying, you'll need to apply for a variation of conditions to your visa.

Before you change your programme of study or your education provider, you'll need to change the conditions of your visa.

Changing your programme of study and/or education provider

How to apply

Complete the application form.

Application for Variation of Conditions form – INZ 1020 (PDF 212KB)

Application fees

Gather supporting documents

You'll also need to provide evidence that:

  • you've been offered a place by another New Zealand education provider
  • you have enough money to live on while you're in NZ
  • you have somewhere to live while you're studying
  • you've paid, or are exempt from paying, the tuition fees
  • you have a ticket out of NZ, or the money to pay for one.

Variation of conditions checklist

Submit your application

Send your application form and documents to your nearest Immigration NZ office.
