Translating official documents

Get an official translation so you can use a New Zealand government document, like a birth certificate, overseas.

How to order 

If you only need a translation send or email the document you need translated to the Department of Internal Affairs' translation service. You don't need to send the original document — a copy is fine.

It costs $85 to translate an NZ-issued:

  • birth certificate
  • death certificate
  • marriage certificate
  • police clearance certificate.

For other documents, you'll be emailed a quote when you send your documents.

There's no form to complete, but include a note that says:

  • what you need
  • your contact details
  • how you want to pay
  • how you'd like the translation returned to you (by post, email or courier — to get it couriered, include a self-addressed prepaid courier bag, or an extra $5).
  • how to spell personal names if the document is being translated into a non-Latin script.

Languages your documents can be translated into

If you also need an apostille or authentication for a document

Depending on the country you're using a document in, you may also need an apostille or authentication to show that the document is official. If you do, you can ask them to arrange translation for you at the same time.

Paying for translations

You need to include payment information when you send your documents. You can pay by:

  • Visa, MasterCard or American Express — include your card details when you send your documents or include a phone number if you want to give your card details over the phone
  • cheque made out to the Department of Internal Affairs
  • money order
  • EFTPOS or cash if you order your translation in person.

If you're a company

Companies ordering translations can also choose to be invoiced after the translation is complete. If you want to be invoiced, include:

  • either your postal address or the email address for your finance department
  • any reference you'd like on the invoice.

What happens next

Translations of standard New Zealand certificates will be returned to you after 1–4 working days. Other documents may take longer.
