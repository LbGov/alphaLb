Are you a Samoan citizen?

If you are a Samoan citizen, you may be able to become an NZ citizen

You can become an NZ citizen if:

  • you were in NZ on 14 September 1982, or
  • you came here after 14 September 1982 and have the right to be here indefinitely.

What kind of New Zealand citizenship did your parent have?
A citizen by grant is someone who moved to NZ and was granted citizenship as an immigrant. A citizen by descent is someone who was born outside NZ and has at least 1 parent who is an NZ citizen.
