Options: simplified fee structure

Option two: A simplified fee structure enabling similar products to have identical pricing

Under this option, Births, Deaths and Marriages (BDM) products use a simplified fee structure. Products requiring similar levels of effort to produce, and holding similar value, have identical fees.  This enables the Department to recover costs, whilst giving consideration to customers who may otherwise be disproportionately disadvantaged. It also takes into account customers who may obtain two or more BDM products for a similar purpose (for example, a name change by statutory declaration and sexual assignment information).

Additionally, similar to Option one, new fees are introduced for those seeking to be appointed as an independent celebrant or a celebrant of an approved organisation, and for the renewal of these registrations. One fee will allow a celebrant to perform both marriages and civil unions.

The BDM memorandum account trends to a positive balance in 2018/19 and the Marriage memorandum account trends to a positive balance in 2019/20.
