Feedback on beta.data.govt.nz

The Department of Internal Affairs (DIA) wants your feedback to help improve beta.data.govt.nz.

We’ll publish your moderated feedback on this page within 3 working days. Your name will be included with your feedback. If you don't want your name published, leave the field blank.

Please tick the box and include your email address if you want to attend the user forums that we’ll be holding in July and August 2016.

Read more about how we treat your information on the terms of use and privacy statement pages.

Very limited export formats compared with Landonline. Why CSV??? This is the lowest common denominator I know, but also the worst for exchanging data.
