Thanks for feedback - we are actively working through some bugs and hope to release some new functionality on site very soon. Specific to recent feedback - the map functionality should be working now, using OpenStreetMap. For now, please use this feedback mechanism to report bugs and we will look into as soon as possible. We are currently looking at aligning some functionality of the current live site with the beta site including data request/reuse function.

The data.govt.nz team | 15 June 2016

Thank you for your feedback, and we will be working through some of the issues raised here throughout the course of beta. We recognise that we have a limited set of datasets represented here (please go to the live site for current full listings) and we are working on a graduated migration, test, and release process that will see more quality dataset types listed here over the coming weeks. We shall also be conducting user testing, analytics review, and engagement through forums etc. to determine where improvements may be made for our different user types.We will also be looking at formats and functionality throughout beta to see where improvements may be made.
