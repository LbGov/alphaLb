Archives 2057 Discussion Document

Archives New Zealand needs to plan for the long term to ensure that the record of government is available to New Zealanders now and in the future. Your input will help Archives develop a long-term strategy.

This survey is now closed to submissions.

Of all national assets, archives are the most precious: they are the gift of one generation to another, and the extent of our care of them marks the extent of our civilization.

Sir Arthur Doughty, Dominion Archivist (Canada)

This consultation document is provided in both English (below) and te reo Māori.

To subscribe for updates about this consultation, complete the "Subscribe to get email updates" box on the left side of this page.

To contact us directly about this consultation, send an email to Archives2057@dia.govt.nz.
