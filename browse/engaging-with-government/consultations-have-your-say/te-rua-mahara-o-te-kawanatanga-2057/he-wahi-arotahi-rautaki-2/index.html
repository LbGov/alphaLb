Wāhi arotahi rautaki 2

Te tuwhera o te kāwanatanga mā te whai wāhi atu mai i te waihangatanga o te kōrero

Me tino pono ki ngā rokiroki kōrero a te kāwanatanga mō te whai wāhi i roto i tētahi manapori me tētahi kāwanatanga tuwhera. Ka taea e ngā āhuatanga pūnaha mamati te āta huri atu mai i ngā punaha tono kōrero ōkawa whakautuutu onāianei ki tētahi pūnaha urupare, wawe ake e taea ai te rere pai o ngā kōrero. Me ū ngā tikanga āta arowhai me te tirotiro mō tēnei e pā ana ki te tūmataiti me te haumaru. Hei tau te tau 2057, ehara ko ngā ratonga me ngā whakahaerenga a te kāwanatanga ka mamati noa, engari ka taea anō pea e te tangata te toro atu ki tōna anō hītori whānui me te kāwanatanga. Mā te arohaehae he aha ngā mea ka puritia, ā, kia hia te roa, ka mutu he aha ka whakangarohia atu, ngā tino mahi mō ngā take pūtea, taunakitanga, haepapa, ahurea me te tuku ihotanga. E hiahiatia ana ngā aromatawai rokiroki mamati tōmua kia wātea mai ai ngā kōrero mō te wā roa e tika ana.

Horopaki

  • Kāore i te mōhiotia ka pēhea te rerekē haere o ngā waiaro takitahi, takimaha hoki ki te tūmataitinga o ngā tapuwae mamati atu ki te 2057.  Engari, ko te whakapae ka hiahia ngā tāngata ki te whakahaere i ā rātau raraunga whaiaro i te wā tonu e piki haere ana ngā hiahia kia tuwhera ake te kāwanatanga me te nui ake o te whakawhitiwhiti ki te iwi whānui. 
  • Ka uaua ake ngā whakatau whakapaunga a te Kāwanatanga nā te iti ake o te ohu mahi whai hua e tautoko ana i tētahi taupori pakeke ake i te tau 2057 ka mutu ka nui ake te hiahia kia whakaritehia ngā kaupapa here uaua me te whakarato uara ōhanga mā te whakamahi i ngā raraunga me ngā kōrero a te kāwanatanga.1
  • Mā te hangarau ka taea te tuku māmā me te "toro atu mai i te wāhi i hangaia".  Ko te whakapātaritari ko te whakaū ka wātea tonu mai ngā rokiroki kōrero whai uara i roto i te wā.  I tēnei wā kei ngā tari anō te kawenga mō te whakatakoto rāhui āhei atu mō ā rātau rokiroki kōrero, ā, ka whai pānga ngā aronga rerekē ki te kaha o ngā kiritaki ki te whakamahi me te whakamahi anō i ngā kōrero a te kāwanatanga. 
  • Kei roto a Aotearoa i te rūnanga whenua Open Government Partnership e mahi ana ki te whakapai ake i te tuwheratanga, te mārama me te urupare a te kāwanatanga.  He tohu hira hoki te Corruption Perceptions Index mō te tuwhera o te kāwanatanga, ā, i te tau 2015 ko Aotearoa te rāngai tūmatanui tuawhā mō te iti o te hēnga i te ao.  Engari, i kī a Transparency International New Zealand, "Ka heke haere tonu ngā tatau o Aotearoa ki te kore e whai haere tonu te tuwhera o ngā kōrero me te kāwana i te whenua ki ngā ia i ngā whenua o te raki o Uropi".2
