He āhuatanga anamata e taea ana

I tētahi ao takenga-mamati ā te tau 2057, he mea taketake tētahi aromatawai tōmua i roto i te "ao" o ngā rokiroki mamati, ā, ka taea te whakaaunoa mā te hoahoa pūnaha pai.  I tua kē atu i te whai pūranga mamati kāwanatanga kotahi, ka noho pea ngā rokiroki kōrero whai uara wā roa ki ngā wāhi maha.  Ka huria e tēnei te āhuatanga o te whakahaere i te whai wāhi atu, engari ka hira tonu te pono ki te "rokiroki kōrero" me te hopu i te horopaki me te kounga o ngā rokiroki.  Ka tino hira te pono ki te whānuitanga o te pūnaha mamati (hanganga pūnaha me ngā tukanga), ā, kei roto hoki ko te whakapūmau i ngā hiahia tūmataiti me te haumaru, ngā whakahaere me ngā parenga o ngā raraunga i wāhi kē, me ngā raraunga wā roa, ngā whakahaere kōrero me ngā rokiroki.   

Ko te whakapae ka riro tonu mā mātau e tiaki, e rokiroki hoki, ngā puringa ōkiko kei a mātau.  Ko te tikanga o tēnei ko te whai pūkenga atawhai, rokiroki hangarau hoki hei whakahaere i ngā puringa ōkiko.  I te mea ko ngā ratonga mamati te tikanga matua mō te torotoro rokiroki kōrero i te tau 2057, ka heke haere pea ngā tono mō ngā ratonga torotoro ōkiko i roto i te wā tērā pea ka whakakotahihia ngā putunga ōkiko.  E tūmanako ana mātau he wāhanga tonu mō tētahi wheako ōkiko o ngā pūranga ā te tau 2057, engari nā te wātea ā-mamati o ngā kōrero ka eke pea ngā hiahia o te nuinga o ngā kaiwhakamahi ka mutu ka wātea mai ngā kōrero ki te maha ake o ngā tāngata.  E āhukahuka ana mātau ka haere tonu ētahi whai wāhi ōkiko, hei tauira, mō ngā rangahau hītori matawhānui, mō ngā take ahurea rānei.

I te tipu haeretanga o ngā reanga o ngā hapori kaiheke ki Aotearoa, ka hāngai haere ngā rokiroki kōrero a te kāwanatanga ki a rātau anō.  I taua wā anō, ka tino rerekē ngā whakamahinga i waenga i ngā reanga me ngā tūmanako hoki mō ngā hangarau.  I tāwāhi, ka hira ake pea ngā mahi a Te Rua Mahara o Te Kāwanatanga i te Te Moananui-a-Kiwa i te mea he hiahia tautoko nō ngā whenua o Te Moananui-a-Kiwa hei urupare ki ngā pānga taiao.

Nō reira ka anga atu te titiro ki te tau 2057, kua whakaarohia e mātau ngā ia matua me ngā kaikōkiri hoki me te whakamātautau i ngā āhuatanga anamata rerekē tērā pea ka puta e mārama ai mātau ki ngā huatau noa ka puta ki ngā anamata maha.  E whakarāpopotohia ana ēnei kaupapa i roto i ngā wāhi arotahi rautaki hei urupare māu.

Nō te tuhinga matapaki Te Rua Mahara o Te Kāwanatanga ēnei whārangi.  E whakaratohia anō tēnei tuhinga ki te hōputu MS Word (.doc 13MP) me te hōputu tuhinga kawe (.pdf 8MP).
