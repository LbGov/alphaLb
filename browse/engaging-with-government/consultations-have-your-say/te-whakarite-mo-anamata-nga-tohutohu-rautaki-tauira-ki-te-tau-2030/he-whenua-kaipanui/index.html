He whenua kaipānui

Te waihanga i tētahi whenua kaipānui whai pūkenga mō te ao hou hei waihanga i te whakahonohono pāpori, whai wāhitanga me te oranga.

Horopaki

He kaha ngā taunakitanga e hāngai ana ki te pānui mō te pārekareka ki te whanaketanga ā-tuhi me ngā momohotanga mātauranga, mahi hoki.1

Ko tōna waru ōrau o ngā tamariki o Aotearoa kei ngā taumata pāpaku rawa mō te kōrero pukapuka.2 Neke atu i te 40% o te hunga kei te 16-65 taipakeke kāore i te whai pūkenga kōrero pukapuka, tātai hoki e pai ai te whai wāhi mai ki tētahi ōhanga whakaputaranga teitei, utu teitei; he tino hē ake ngā tatauranga mō te Māori me ō Te Moananui-a-Kiwa.3 Ko te hunga i whānau mai i waho o Aotearoa he 38% rātau i roto i ngā tatau kōrero pukapuka iti rawa. He tino iti rawa ngā tatau kōrero pukapuka o te hunga mauhere ki tā te iwi whānui.4

Tata ki te $2.9 piriona te utu o te kūare ā-tuhi ki Aotearoa i ia tau, ā, he 30-42% te iti ake o ngā utu a te hunga kūare ā-tuhi ki te hunga mātau ki te tuhi.5 Neke au i te $300 miriona kei te whakapaua ki te whakapakari ake i ngā pūkenga kōrero pukapuka o te hunga pakeke i roto i ngā tau e whā e whai ake.6

Paerangi 1: Te whakapai haere tonu

Ngā whai wāhitanga e kite ana mātau

  • Ka taea te whakamahi ngā mōhio o Te Puna Mātauranga mō te Ratonga ki Ngā Kura me te uru ki ia kura hei takawaenga kia kaha ake te whai pānui

Te āhua pea o te momohotanga

  • Ko ngā kaupapa pēnei i te Summer Reading and Teachers as Readers i whakatūhia e Te Kaunihera Pukapuka o Aotearoa, ngā whare pukapuka tūmatanui me ngā pātui kāwanatanga hei āwhina i ngā kaiako ki te whakaawe i ngā mahi pānui; tae atu ki tētahi kaupapa kōrero pukapuka i Te Tonga o Tāmaki Makaurau e aro ana ki ngā taumata kōrero pukapuka o ngā iwi o Te Moananui-a-Kiwa

  • I tohua te New Zealand Children’s Laureate hei taunaki i te painga o te pānui me ōna hua mātauranga, pāpori, ahurea me te ōhanga

Paerangi 2: Ahunga hou

Ngā whai wāhitanga e kite ana mātau

  • Ka taea e te tūhononga i waenga i ngā rauemi tōpū a ngā whare pukapuka te whakakaha ngā taumata kōrero pukapuka, whakawhiti hoki puta noa i ngā hapori o Aotearoa

  • Te hāpai i ngā mahi pānui hei urupare ki ngā uauatanga kōrero pukapuka o te hunga pakeke

Te āhua pea o te momohotanga

  • He pātuitanga i waenga tari kāwanatanga, kura, te rāngai mātauranga whānui me ngā whare pukapuka tūmatanui e whai ana i tētahi whakapaitanga nui ki te maha o ngā tāngata o Aotearoa he rawaka te taumata kōrero pukapuka mā te pānu

  • He rēhita mema whare pukapuka tūmatanui i te whānautanga mai me ngā kura kōhungahunga, kura rānei

  • Kei te whai pātui hei tohatoha rauemi tuhinga reo Māori mamati mā ngā purere ā-hiko ki ia ākonga reo Māori i ngā kura

Paerangi 3: Hōparatanga me te hurihanga

Ngā whai wāhitanga e kite ana mātau

  • Te whakakaha kaiārahitanga puta noa i ngā rāngai kāwanatanga, mātauranga me te whare pukapuka hei whakapiki i te taumata kōrero pukapuka o ngā tāngata o Aotearoa

Te āhua pea o te momohotanga

  • Ka hōrapatia whānuitia te ratauki pānui ā-motu i Aotearoa me ngā pātui i ahu mai i te momohotanga o ngā kaupapa me ngā pātuitanga kōrero pukapuka hou

  • E pūmau kaha ana ki whakatutuki i ngā putanga pāpori mau tonu, ahurea, ōhanga hoki mā ngā hapori mā te whakatairanga i ngā mahi pānui

Te whakarite mō anamata

Whatunga mōhiotanga kotahi: Te huri i te āhua o tā tātau tiri mōhiotanga hei whakaū i te urunga māmā o ngā tāngata o Aotearoa ki ngā kohinga me ngā rangahau tūmatanui e puritia ana.

He whenua kaipānui: Te waihanga i tētahi whenua kaipānui whai pūkenga mō te ao hou hei waihanga i te whakahonohono pāpori, whai wāhitanga me te oranga.

Ko ngā kupu hei taonga: Ka uara ngā tāngata o Aotearoa i ngā kupu, oro me ngā pikitia i waihangatia, i kohia hoki hei tuhinga tuku iho mā tātau, kia whiwhi mōhio ai tātau mai i neherā me te whakahihiri i tō tātau anamata.

Questions/Ngā pātai: He mea waiwai tō urupare hei waihanga i tō mātau huarahi atu ki 2030, e whakarite ana e hāngai ana ā mātau kaupapa, ratonga hoki e marohitia ana ki ngā kaingākautanga, hiahia, kaha hoki o te hunga e paheko ana ki a mātau.
