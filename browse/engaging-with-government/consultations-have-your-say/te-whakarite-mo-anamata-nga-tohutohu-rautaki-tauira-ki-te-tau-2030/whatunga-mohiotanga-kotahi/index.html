Whatunga mōhiotanga kotahi

Te huri i te āhua o tā tātau tiri mōhiotanga hei whakaū i te urunga māmā o ngā tāngata o Aotearoa ki ngā kohinga me ngā rangahau tūmatanui e puritia ana.

Horopaki

Kua mahue te whakaputaranga me te tipuranga hoko ki tāwāhi o Aotearoa i ētahi atu whenua OECD. Ka taea e te auahatanga te whakapiki i te whakaputaranga me te huaki i ngā mākete hou. Ko ngā mōhiotanga hika auahatanga ka ahu mai i ngā kaimahi, kiritaki, kaiwhakarato hoki, engari ka ahu mai anō i ngā pukapuka, hautaka, whakapuakinga whakamana ā-ture, ipurangi hoki.1

Kei te tere tonu te tipu haere anō o te ipurangi, me te aha tērā pea ka puhake rawa atu i roto i ngā tau tekau mā rima e whai ake. O te taupori tapeke o Aotearoa i te tau 2015, 91.5% he kaiwhakamahi ipurangi, ā, 99% i raro i ngā tau 40 he kaiwhakamahi ipurangi.2

I te tau ki te Pipiri 2015, i pūrongo ngā whare pukapuka tūmatanui o Aotearoa i ngā kaiwhakamahi rēhita neke atu i te rua miriona, tata ki te 42 miriona ngā tono ki ngā kohinga o ngā tuemi 12 miriona me ngā ī-pukapuka 400,000.3 E whakaratohia ana e ia kaunihera ā-takiwā he ratonga whare pukapuka.

Tata ki te 14% o ngā mahinga i whakaputaina i Aotearoa (i whakaputaina “e, mā, mō” Aotearoa) e wātea tuihonotia ana. O ēnei, he maha kei te tauāraitia te whai wāhi atu nā ngā pātū utu, hono pakaru, me rēhita rānei.4

Paerangi 1: Te whakapai haere tonu

Ngā whai wāhitanga e kite ana mātau

  • E kaha ana te whakawhirinaki o te iwi whānui ki ngā whare pukapuka tūmatanui, kura, whare wānanga hoki me te tino whai wāhi hoki o te iwi ki ēnei, ā, ka taea tēnei te whakamahi hei āwhina i te whakawhitinga whakaputanga mamati

Te āhua pea o te momohotanga

  • Ngā kirimana ā-motu mō te raihana ī-ihirangi me ngā pūnaha hou kia taea ai te uru whānui atu mā ngā whare pukapuka tūmatanui katoa, whare pukapuka kura hoki

Paerangi 2: Ahunga hou

Ngā whai wāhitanga e kite ana mātau

  • Kotahi, neke atu rānei ngā otinga whatunga e whakaratohia ana e Te Puna Mātauranga kei te whakamahia e ngā whare pukapuka neke atu i te 90 ōrau o Aotearoa hei tuku urunga ki ā rātau rauemi

Te āhua pea o te momohotanga

  • Ka whai wāhi ngā kaunihera me ngā kura ki tētahi rēhita mema whare pukapuka me te pūnaha whakahaerenga i Aotearoa whānui, te waihanga i tētahi whatunga whare pukapuka kotahi

  • Kua tino hīkina ake te mamatitanga o ngā ihirangi o Aotearoa, tae atu ki te waihangatanga o ngā metararaunga e hiahiatia ana kia taea ai te uru taunoa atu ki ngā ihirangi mamati

  • Te paetukutuku i hangaia houtia, tae atu ki te whaitaketanga pai ake me te whakaatu ihirangi kia pai ai te pāhekoheko me ngā kohinga tuihono a Te Puna Mātauranga, me ngā kohinga a ngā whakaurunga pātui

Paerangi 3: Hōparatanga me te hurihanga

Ngā whai wāhitanga e kite ana mātau

  • He 4.5 miriona te taupori, nā te iti ka taea e Aotearoa te auaha pēnei tonu I tētahi tāone nui mā ngā whatunga tuwhera, mahi tahi hoki

Te āhua pea o te momohotanga

  • Te pātui me ngā kaiwhakaputa, whakahaere hanga kaupapa me ngā rōpū whakahaere tika ki te hōpara me pēhea te whakawātea mai ā-mamati i ngā putanga katoa e whakaputaina i Aotearoa ki ngā tāngata o Aotearoa. He tauira pea te tikanga a Nōwei mō tēnei momo pātui.5

  • Te tūhono i te whatunga whare pukapuka o Aotearoa hei hono ki ngā kohinga puta noa i ngā rāngai ahurea, mātauranga, rangahau, kāwanatanga hoki

Te whakarite mō anamata

Whatunga mōhiotanga kotahi: Te huri i te āhua o tā tātau tiri mōhiotanga hei whakaū i te urunga māmā o ngā tāngata o Aotearoa ki ngā kohinga me ngā rangahau tūmatanui e puritia ana.

He whenua kaipānui: Te waihanga i tētahi whenua kaipānui whai pūkenga mō te ao hou hei waihanga i te whakahonohono pāpori, whai wāhitanga me te oranga.

Ko ngā kupu hei taonga: Ka uara ngā tāngata o Aotearoa i ngā kupu, oro me ngā pikitia i waihangatia, i kohia hoki hei tuhinga tuku iho mā tātau, kia whiwhi mōhio ai tātau mai i neherā me te whakahihiri i tō tātau anamata.

Questions/Ngā pātai: He mea waiwai tō urupare hei waihanga i tō mātau huarahi atu ki 2030, e whakarite ana e hāngai ana ā mātau kaupapa, ratonga hoki e marohitia ana ki ngā kaingākautanga, hiahia, kaha hoki o te hunga e paheko ana ki a mātau.
