Open Government Partnership Action Plan

This survey is now closed to submissions.

As part of the Government’s commitment to the Open Government Partnership (OGP) the New Zealand Government have now developed their National Action Plan (NAP) for Open Government.

The NAP outlines how we will deliver the OGP principles of transparency, accountability, technology and innovation, and public participation in government.

The NAP was developed in stages with engagement around the introduction of the document to be outlined in the plan managed through this website, and a separate tool used to encourage idea generation around actions that might be included in the Plan. For more information about the engagement process visit the project website: http://engage2.co.nz/ogpnz/

The second National Action Plan for Open Government was released by the New Zealand Government on the 20th October 2016 and published on this website ogp.org.nz.
