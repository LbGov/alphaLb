Grants if you’re moving to Christchurch to work

If you're on a benefit and get a full-time job in the Christchurch area you can apply for a payment of up to $3,000 to help you relocate.

Search for job vacancies

When you're job hunting you need to treat it like a job by:

  • planning your search
  • writing a to-do list
  • checking job adverts online and in newspapers
  • contacting a business you want to work for and ask them about job vacancies they might have.
